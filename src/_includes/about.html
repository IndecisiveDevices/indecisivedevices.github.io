About Indecisive Devices

Established in 2024, Indecisive Devices is a passionate team of students dedicated to robotics and innovation. We are learning, innovating, competing, and (mostly) deciding on a design!

We are proud members of FIRST (For Inspiration and Recognition of Science and Technology), and we qualified for the Michigan State Competition in the 2025-2026 season.

Our mission is to build exceptional robots, develop valuable skills in engineering and teamwork, and inspire the next generation of innovators.

What is FIRST?

FIRST is a global STEM competition organization that engages students and mentors in hands-on robotics challenges, fostering innovation and leadership.
